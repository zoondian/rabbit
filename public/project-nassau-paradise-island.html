Nassau Paradise Island

Working closely with the Nassau Paradise Island Promotion Board, the GOCO created short form and long form national television commercials, Tru-View web spots and other multi-channel assets that have made NassauParadiseIsland.com the most visited portal for vacations in the Bahamas.

Branded Direct Response

Our work for the Nassau Paradise Island Promotion Board allows us to apply our DR experience to destination marketing. We convey the desirable experience of a vacation in The Bahamas as an accessible, attainable lifestyle, then combine that with a compelling offer. In effect, we merged a brand statement with the urgency of a retail message.
