Process

The five GOCO principles of customer engagement

At the GOCO we believe all successful customer engagement projects — regardless of medium, venue, or audience — click any of the five overarching process principles to learn more:

Transparency

Transparency

Transparency of Goals & Challenges.

Success begins and ends with a clear definition and mutual agreement on what constitutes success. How will success be measured? What results will define success? These questions must be answered at the outset, for they will be the foundation for all creative, strategic, and production decisions.

Measurement

Measurement

Measurement & Iterative Improvement.

We believe that even the best campaigns, projects, and experiences can benefit from iterative enhancements. As our clients' stories continue to evolve and grow, every brand, story, product and experience must also continue to being enhanced and improved. The GOCO works closely with our clients to measure, analyze and interpret where improvements can be made to further deepen customer engagement and increase ROI.

Clarity

Clarity

Clarity of Audience & Message.

A clear and shared understanding of the intended audience is a primary requirement for developing the content and the creative approach most likely to engage the target audience. We work closely with our clients to develop "customer personas" to help identify different customer types, their values, their motivations, and their reasons for engaging with a client's story.

Collaboration

Collaboration

Collaboration & Innovation.

The best ideas and innovations come from the collaboration between people offering a variety of skill sets, backgrounds, and points-of-view. We use frequent in-person meetings and on-line collaboration tools to bring clients, the GoCo, and our strategic partner firms together to generate breakthrough insights and ideas.

Execution

Execution

Planning & Execution.

We build a plan that is executable and actionable given the considerations of budget, timing, and success criteria. Innovative ideas are only valuable if they can be achieved within the parameters of a project. We work closely with our clients to define those parameters early so everyone involved knows the limits of schedule and budget that must be maintained.
